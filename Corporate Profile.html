Company Profile

HARNESSING THE INVISIBLE
POWER OF THE WIND FOR HUMAN PROGRESS:


Kintech Synergy Private Limited (KSPL) is the Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) Company established in 1995 engaged in the business of turnkey wind power solutions like wind farm development, wind power generation and wind turbine installation and maintenance services. KINTECH is an ISO Certified Company which offer technical consultation and project execution in the field of wind power generation and distribution. Kintech Synergy is leading Service provider and has more than two decade of experience of wind mill Operation and Maintenance of 55KW to 2MW Class WTG & BOP Maintenance of MW Class WTG, company are seeking to expand their operations role across the India.

 

The Company is a certified channel partner for empanelment under Grid Connected Rooftop and Small Solar Power Plants Programmer awarded by Ministry of New and Renewable Energy.

 

  • Getting across Gujarat, Rajasthan, MP, Maharashtra, Karnataka and Tamilnadu states in India
  • 1300+ employees on pay roll
  • Capable to provide concept to commissioning solution for wind farm
  • In house capacity to Operate and Maintain the Wind farm as an independent service provider
  • Members of InWEA, IWPA and WEPA (Wind Energy Associations in India)
