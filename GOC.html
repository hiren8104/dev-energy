Group Companies

Kintech Global Services Private Limited


KINTECH GLOBAL SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED (KGSPL) is a Wholly Owned Subsidiary Company of Kintech Synergy Private Limited registered under Companies Act, 2013. KGSPL is a Service Provider Company engaged in the business of providing consultation, installation, operations & maintenance and services in the field of energy and to facilitate Research and analysis and Turnkey projects in the field of Renewable energy. The Company is also engaged in the business of Purchase, Sale, Lease, Sub-Lease and Rent of land for Solar Power Plant, Wind Energy and Renewable Energy and Other Power Solutions.

RAJANA SOLARPARK PRIVATE LIMITED (RSPPL)


RAJANA SOLARPARK PRIVATE LIMITED (RSPPL) is a Wholly Owned Subsidiary Company of Kintech Synergy Private Limited registered under Companies Act, 2013. RSPPL is involved in the field of sales, Services and maintenance of Hi tech industrial and electronic systems and Renewable Power Generation and infrastructure of Windfarm Project execution work.
